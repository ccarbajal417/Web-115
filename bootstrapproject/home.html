How to wash your car!

In this website it is going to explain the basic steps to washing your car. Something that is labeled as so simple yet, many people cannot seem to get the process right. With these simple steps, one will never make a mistake with washing their car again.

How difficult can it really be to have a clean car people ask?

People fail to realize that in order to get a decent car wash they have to do it themselves. The process of going into a drive-in car wash is not only overpriced, but in terms of cleaning the car, barely works. The more efficient way of washing your car would be to power wash it yourself. The price is dramatically reduced by more than half in doing so as well.

Step-by-Step basic wash.

Step 1: Go into local car wash and enter the 'self wash' bay.
Step 2: Apply any products you may have for your wheels, mirrors, etc. Place car mats on hangers and get ready for the wash.
Step 3: Wash your car with the powerwasher, starting with a rinse, then pressurised soap, and finally a rinse to finish it off again. If there's the option for a wax finish, after the final rinse use the wax.
Step 4: Find a shaded spot to wipe the water off your car, the trick is to have two rags to avoid any water marks in the end. You will need a rag to wipe the water off (wet rag), also a rag to wipe off the smears of the wet rag so there are no water marks at all.
