Now that we have explained how to do a basic car wash next we will explain how to do a more detailed complex car wash.

Down below are the steps and products on how to do the more detailed car wash.

Products to help wash your vehicle.

Top Coat Products. Adam's polishes products. Turtle Wax's Products.

Detailed Wash Tuorial.

CarWashPro Steps:
Step 1: Use a soap gun and spray the entire vehicle. Let the soap soak in for a couple of minutes.
Step 2: Rinse the entire car with a power washer for first clean.
Step 3: Then, use a foam brush to scrub the car entirely and get that excess harder dirt off.
Step 4: Grab the power washer again and rinse it off. Wax finish is the best for finish but is not completely necessary.
Step 5: Finally, go to shaded area, and grab your two rags the dry and moist rag and use them to dry off the entire car and you should be finished.
